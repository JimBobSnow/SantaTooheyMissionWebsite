Welcome to Santa Toohey Mission

Inspired by Santa Claus, I want to bring kindness, generosity, and long-term freedom to others through community projects, volunteer connecting, and hands-on help.

About Me

I’ve wanted to be like Santa Claus ever since I was little. Now I'm making that dream real by helping communities and individuals. When I have free time or am unable to fund my own projects, I spend it volunteering to support others with theirs.

This is not an official charity and I do not collect any donations.

Community Projects

I’ve taken a shift in my approach to helping others. In the past, I’ve given away Christmas presents and hosted meal giveaways, but now I focus on providing resources that offer long-term benefits for the people who receive them. At the moment, my efforts are centered on school-related needs, but I’m always open to addressing other community challenges and individual problems.

Volunteering

I’m always looking for individuals who can lend a hand, as my own resources are limited. My goal is to build a team of volunteers who can not only help me with my projects but also assist others with theirs. I’ll never share your information. If I think you're a good match for one of my projects or someone else's, I’ll contact you directly.
